The Story

Where Latitude
Marketing came from.

Latitude Marketing was founded by Luke McGrath — a Sydney-based marketer with experience across some of Australia's most recognised organisations in sport and entertainment, including the Cronulla Sharks NRL club and the Australian Turf Club.

That background built something you can't teach: a commercial instinct for what works, what doesn't, and how to cut through noise in a competitive market.

Latitude Marketing was built to bring that same level of strategic thinking to local Sydney businesses — tradies, service providers, retailers, hospitality — businesses with real potential that simply aren't being seen.

The agency is deliberately lean. No layers, no account managers, no juniors handling your work. You deal directly with the person who built the strategy and executes it.

How We Work

The principles behind
every decision we make.

01
Visibility first.

Before any content is created or any ad is launched, we identify where your business is losing attention online. Everything flows from there — not the other way around.

02
Clarity over complexity.

We don't use jargon. We don't over-report. We tell you what's working, what isn't, and what we're doing about it. Always in plain English.

03
Results, not activity.

Posting more is not a strategy. Spending more is not a strategy. We tie every action back to one outcome: enquiries that turn into revenue.

04
Local first.

We're based in the Sutherland Shire and we work with Sydney businesses. Local knowledge isn't a talking point — it's how we build campaigns that actually connect.

05
Direct access — always.

No account managers. No juniors. No handoffs. The person who built your strategy is the person running it. You always know who's responsible.

06
Build lean, grow smart.

We recommend what you need — not what generates the biggest invoice. Start with the foundation, prove it works, then scale from a position of confidence.

Our Approach

How we think about
your marketing.

01
Find the gap first

We start every engagement with a visibility audit. Before recommending anything, we look at where you're losing attention — website, social, search — and rank what will have the biggest impact.

02
Build the foundation

The website comes first. If it doesn't convert, nothing else you do online will perform the way it should. We don't layer content or ads on top of a broken foundation.

03
Add reach with content

Once the foundation is right, consistent and strategic content builds trust with your audience over time. This is the long game — and it compounds.

04
Scale with paid ads

Paid advertising is the accelerator. When the system is in place, ads put your business in front of people who don't know you yet — at scale, with precision.
