Common Questions

Frequently asked.

How long does a website build take?+
Most website builds are completed within 3–4 weeks from strategy to launch. More complex projects may take longer — we'll give you a clear timeline upfront.
Do I need all three services?+
Not necessarily at once. Most clients start with a website, then layer in content or ads once the foundation is right. We'll recommend what makes sense for your situation after the free audit.
What's the minimum ad budget I need?+
For Meta Ads, we recommend a minimum of $500–$1,000/month in ad spend to see meaningful results. For Google Ads, $300–$600/month can work well for local service businesses. We're transparent about what's realistic at each budget level.
Do you work with businesses outside Sutherland Shire?+
Yes — we work with businesses across Greater Sydney. Our base is the Sutherland Shire but we work with clients across the city and surrounds.
How do I know if it's working?+
We set clear goals at the start of every engagement and report against them monthly. You'll always know what's running, what it's producing, and what we're doing to improve it.
Who will I be working with?+
Directly with Luke McGrath. No account managers, no handoffs. The person who built the strategy is the person running it.
